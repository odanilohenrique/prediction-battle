# 🚀 Prediction Battle - DApp For Sale

## 📖 The Pitch
**Prediction Battle** is a fully functional, web3-native decentralized application (DApp) that allows users to place USDC wagers on the real-world performance of Farcaster casts and social media engagement. 

Designed for the booming SocialFi and prediction market sectors (like Polymarket), Prediction Battle brings the thrill of speculation to social media. Will a post hit 100 likes in 24 hours? Will a specific user get ratioed? Users can bet on it.

## 🌟 Why Buy Prediction Battle?
- **Ready to Launch:** The smart contracts are audited implicitly through rigorous testing and deployed. The frontend is live on Vercel. 
- **High Retention Loop:** Social betting creates natural virality. Users want to share the bets they make on their friends' or influencers' posts.
- **Built-in Monetization:** The smart contract enforces a strict **10% house fee** on every resolved market. The platform generates revenue automatically with zero ongoing maintenance.
- **Cross-Platform Potential:** While currently integrated with Farcaster, the architecture allows easy expansion to X (Twitter), Lens Protocol, or generic real-world events.

## 🛠️ Tech Stack
- **Frontend:** Next.js 14, React, TailwindCSS, TypeScript
- **Web3 Integration:** Wagmi, Viem, OnchainKit
- **Smart Contracts:** Solidity (V10, highly optimized, MEV-protected)
- **Database/Cache:** Vercel KV (Redis)
- **Social Graph:** Farcaster Protocol Integration

## ✨ Core Features
- **Decentralized Escrow:** All funds (USDC) are held securely in the `PredictionBattleV10` smart contract on the Base network.
- **Admin Dashboard:** A comprehensive, authenticated admin panel to monitor active bets, view liquidity, and resolve markets manually or automatically.
- **Dynamic Odds & Liquidity:** Parimutuel betting system where the odds are dynamically calculated based on the pool size of YES vs NO bettors.
- **Open-Ended Markets:** Support for both fixed-deadline bets (e.g., "in 24 hours") and open-ended bets (e.g., "first to reach 1000 followers").
- **MEV Protection:** Built-in 5-minute cooldowns prevent malicious actors from seeing an event happen off-chain and front-running the resolution on-chain.

## 💰 Monetization Model
The DApp is designed to be a cash-flow positive asset from day one of mainnet launch:
1. **House Edge:** 10% of the total betting pool is routed to the designated Treasury Wallet upon market resolution.
2. **Referral System:** 5% of the pool can be routed to referrers, encouraging organic growth.
3. **Creator Economy:** 5% of the pool goes to the market creator, incentivizing influencers to create markets around their own content.
4. **Winner takes the rest:** The remaining 80% is distributed to the winning side proportionally to their stake.
